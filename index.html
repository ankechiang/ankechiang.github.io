Bio: Meng-Fen is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Electronics and Electrical Engineering (ECE) at National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University (NYCU). Prior to joining NYCU, she was a Lecturer in Computer Science at The University of Auckland. She received her Ph.D. in Computer Science at National Chiao-Tung University in Taiwan. From 2014 to 2019, she worked at the Living Analytics Research Centre at Singapore Management University as a Researcher and an Adjunct Professor. Her research focuses on the intersection of multimodal representation learning and computational linguistics.
