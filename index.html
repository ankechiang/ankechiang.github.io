Meng-Fen (Anke) Chiang is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Electronics and Electrical Engineering (ECE) at National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University (NYCU). Her research focuses on Reliable Multimodal Intelligence, spanning Large Language Models (LLMs), Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), and Responsible generative AI.

Prior to NYCU, she was a Lecturer at The University of Auckland and a Researcher/Adjunct Professor at the Living Analytics Research Centre in Singapore. She received her Ph.D. from National Chiao-Tung University.
